How to use Replay mode:
Pick a pre-loaded Wooster game or paste in your own play-by-play text (copy from any SIDEARM box score page). Hit Play and the app will read each play aloud with a pause between them — great for a long drive.

How to use Live mode:
Enter the SIDEARM stats URL for the home team. The app polls every 20 seconds and announces new plays. Works best on Ohio Wesleyan's site (Apr 11) since it links directly from Wooster's schedule.
