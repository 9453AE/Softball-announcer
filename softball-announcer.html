How this works:
This app polls the SIDEARM live stats page every 20 seconds, reads the play-by-play data, and speaks new plays aloud using your phone's built-in voice — so you can listen while driving.

Steps:
1. Go to Setup and paste the game URL
2. Enter the team names
3. Tap "▶ Start" on the Live Game tab
4. Keep your phone screen on (or use "speak every N seconds" to re-announce)

Important: The SIDEARM page is JavaScript-rendered, so this app reads the play-by-play text that gets embedded in the page. If the proxy fails, try the other proxy option in Setup.

Voice tip: On iPhone, choose "Samantha" for clearest speech. On Android, choose "Google US English."
Troubleshooting

No plays loading? Try switching the proxy in Setup. The SIDEARM site may block certain proxies.

Duplicate plays? Normal — the app tracks what it's already announced.

Voice not working? Tap "Test Voice" first — iOS requires a user gesture to unlock audio.

App stopping? Keep your browser tab active. iOS will pause background tabs after a few minutes.
